  • WHO IS RATANJIT S. SONDHE?

    About Ratanjit

    Internationally renowned as Mr. Stress-Free, Ratanjit S. Sondhe is a professional speaker, author, radio and television personality, consultant, and was the founder and 34-year CEO of a material science company recently acquired by The Dow Chemical Company. He credits all of his worldly success and good fortune to realizing his place in the universe and unconditionally adding value to the bigger one - our society.
     
    DOWNLOAD MEDIA KIT

    American Success Story

    Be proud. Ratanjit is an American success story. He emigrated from India to the U.S. in 1968 with no more than a few dollars in his pocket to complete his Ph.D. studies in Polymer Chemistry. He soon became an entrepreneur, creating POLY-CARB, Inc. on a modern-style leadership paradigm in which team members are directed by the company's mission statement, operating principles, and personal values. 

    Ratanjit's Mission

    Yes, a mission - a mission to give back. Having created tremendous wealth in business, is relationships and his family, and seeing the discontent, stress and depression so prevalent today, Ratanjit realized a desire to give back. And that's what he's now doing. He has embarked on a mission to share what he has learned from his business experience, world travels, reading of countless books, attendance at many lectures and seminars, and most importantly, his interaction with family and friends. He has received international recognition and been honored with top awards from the NRI Institute, SALDEF, AIABG, Kent State University, The American Red Cross, and others. 

    The Causes of Stress and Frustrations

    After many experiences, Ratanjit found that the cause is always the same - it's because we become disconnected from the universal wisdom that exists around us and right within us.

    Reconnecting for Success and True Joy

    Yes, you can get reconnected. You can share the benefits of this way of thinking and you can do it right now. The stories and information in Ratanjit's lectures have helped countless people! Additionally, Ratanjit wrote TEA: The Recipe for Stress-Free Living , The Secret of Our Ultimate Success and Oneness Changes Everything , has assembled a collection of 25 habits that are critical to achieving Stress-Free success, broadcasts his international Stress-Free Living TV show, and hosts a 15-year weekly radio show and daily Quick Thought.

  • Ratanjit as Entrepreneur

    Business is about adding value.

    For Ratanjit, business is about adding value. After emigrating from India to the United States in 1968 to complete his Ph.D. studies in Polymer Chemistry, Ratanjit quickly found himself at home in America, where he saw opportunities plentiful for anyone willing to work hard, take risks, and operate on solid principles.
     
    He worked through many difficulties and founded POLY-CARB, a global multi-million dollar construction and road-building materials corporation that was acquired by The Dow Chemical Company in 2007. POLY-CARB was structured on a modern-style leadership paradigm in which there were no bosses or titles, and team members were given direction by the company’s mission statement, path, and operating principles.

  • Ratanjit as "Mr. Stress-Free"

    Living stress-free...

    "Mr. Stress-Free!" Impossible? Guess again. Through hundreds of challenges and adversities as the founder and CEO of a 34-year material science company, a professional speaker, author, radio and television personality and consultant, Ratanjit S. Sondhe remains stress free.
     
    Upon the release of TEA: The Recipe for Stress-Free Living, Ratanjit quickly became recognized for his work on living stress-free. The inspiration for TEA came during his international television program when Ratanjit was asked how to deal with stress. His response was to simply have a cup of TEA - and the rest is history.
     
    Ratanjit understands that hidden under the millions of stress-causing labels that society has placed on us, we are naturally stress-free. Stress is simply a mechanism, which is really a gift to our human systems, that puts us on alert that something unusual is happening. It's this natural response that gives us a burst of energy and the strength to handle any situation. The problem arises when we remain in this mode for extended periods of time, which happens easily in today's fast-paced society.

    The 25 Habits of a Successful and Flourishing Life

    Heading

    We accumulate knowledge, we seek it, but we do not live our knowledge. Instead, we file it away, talk about, write books about it, and even preach it. But the truth is that we instead live our habits. Until we convert our knowledge into habits, we simply don’t live that knowledge.

     

    We are truly creatures of habits ― they influence and impact our lives, careers, relationships, and decision making. Yet, are they habits that empower and free us or incapacitate and enslave us? Essentially, habits are your identifying characteristics, acquired disciplines, predictable actions, are sometimes inherited, always being formed, liabilities when driven by pleasures, assets when controlled, opportunities for improvement, your loyal servants, and chosen wisely by the successful.

     

    Any time you consecutively practice anything, it becomes a habit. Habits are the means for allowing us to do things with minimal effort and thinking. If we have bad habits, they’ll ultimately get us into trouble. If we have good habits, they become a means for empowerment and success.

     

    Learn the 12 Self-integration and 12 Social-integration habits, and the one Master Habit that will transform and enrich every aspect of your life giving you control in your life, minimizing stress, and enhancing your self-worth, dignity, reliability, and confidence.
